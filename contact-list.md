## Communication Protocol
1. **Pre‑burn coordination**: Contact Air District Manager and Local Fire Chief at least 7 days prior.
2. **Day‑of‑burn notification**: Notify all key contacts by 8:00 AM on ignition day.
3. **Post‑burn follow‑up**: Provide summary report to Air District Manager within 24 hours.
