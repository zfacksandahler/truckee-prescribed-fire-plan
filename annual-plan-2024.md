# Annual Prescribed Fire Operations Plan 2024

## Project Objectives
1. Reduce hazardous fuel loads in designated areas to lower wildfire risk.
2. Promote ecosystem health and biodiversity through habitat restoration.
3. Improve wildlife habitat for species dependent on fire-adapted landscapes.
4. Enhance forest resilience to climate change and future disturbances.
5. Implement burns with minimal smoke impact on nearby communities.

## Proposed Burn Units (list)
- **Unit 1**: Martis Valley - 150 acres
- **Unit 2**: Donner Ridge - 200 acres  
- **Unit 3**: Prosser Creek - 100 acres
- **Unit 4**: Sagehen Creek - 180 acres
- **Unit 5**: Boca Reservoir - 120 acres
- **Unit 6**: Stampede Meadows - 90 acres
